about us

The sea from a distance is the color of sapphire

With 2,000km of coastline and much of it long stretches of white sand, Myanmar offers many unspoiled and undiscovered beaches. The best time to visit Myanmar beaches is December through April with sunny days, warm temperatures (30-32 degrees Celsius) and blue sky. By spring, the water is warm.

The most important thing to remember about the Myanmar beach scene is that resorts close down during the monsoon season. It is from May to October in preparation for the high winds and heavy rainfall that batter the coast annually.
